FAQ

Common Questions

Is this financial advice?

No. This is education and strategy discussion only. We teach frameworks and review portfolios for educational purposes — we never tell you what to buy or sell. All investment decisions are yours.

Do I need trading experience for the course?

No. The course starts from zero. We cover market basics before touching a chart or an options chain.

Can I just get a portfolio review without the course?

Yes. The trading course and portfolio services are independent. You can use either or both. Contact us to discuss your portfolio needs.

Do I need a specific brokerage?

No. Use whichever platform you prefer. We teach concepts and strategies — your brokerage choice is entirely yours. We have no affiliate deals.

What if I miss a live session?

Every session is recorded. You get lifetime access to all recordings. Watch anytime and bring questions to the next live session.

What does the portfolio review include?

We analyze your holdings, account allocation (TFSA/RRSP/Margin), tax efficiency, and suggest options strategies like covered calls for income or puts for protection. We also flag any CRA compliance issues.

Can I get a refund on the course?

If you attend the first session and feel it's not for you, contact us within 24 hours for a full refund. No questions asked.
