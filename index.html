FAQ

Common Questions

Is this financial advice?

No. Ascent Trading Academy is education only. We teach frameworks, strategies, and systems — we never tell you what to buy or sell. All trading decisions are yours. We are not licensed investment advisors. Always consult a qualified financial professional.

Do I need a specific brokerage?

No. Our education is completely broker-neutral. You use whichever platform you prefer. We teach the concepts and mechanics — the execution platform is your choice. We have no affiliate deals with any brokerage.

Do I need a lot of money to start?

No. We strongly recommend paper trading before risking real capital. Our Pathway requires 30 days on a simulator before going live. Start learning with zero capital at risk.

Is this only day trading?

No. We teach swing trading, options strategies, and long-term investing — all unified under one system. Most of our strategies are multi-day or multi-week holds, not intraday scalping.

Why focus on US markets?

US markets (NYSE, NASDAQ) offer the deepest liquidity, the most active options chains, and the widest range of tradable stocks. Canadian brokerages give you full access. We also cover TSX for long-term investing — but active trading happens primarily on US exchanges.

Why is this "for Canadians" specifically?

Because trading US stocks from Canada creates unique challenges: TFSA/RRSP rules on options and US dividends, currency conversion costs, ACB tracking for the CRA, superficial loss rules, W-8BEN requirements. No US-based academy teaches any of this.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no lock-in. Cancel in 2 clicks from your account. If the system isn't delivering value, you shouldn't be paying for it.
